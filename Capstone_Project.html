Our Commitment to Ethics & Privacy

At FarmFresh Kenya, we operate with transparency and respect for both our farmers and customers. We adhere to Kenya's digital laws and ethical guidelines to ensure a safe marketplace for all.

  • Data Protection: Compliant with Kenya's Data Protection Act (2019)
  • Fair Pricing: Transparent pricing with fair compensation to farmers
  • Secure Transactions: Protection in line with Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act (2018)
  • Informed Consent: Clear privacy notices and user consent for data collection
  • Sustainable Practices: Supporting eco-friendly farming methods

We follow guidelines from the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) and National ICT Policy (2019) to ensure ethical digital practices.
